Sebastien has been a skateboarder and snowboarder since he was a child. He has lived in many different skitowns around the world and often travels to find new skate communities. He is always searching for the ultimate ride.

Seb is an active explorer, that loves wild food foraging and the natural world. He engages in many remote hiking adventures, canoe trips and splitboarding missions. He is familiar with navigation techniques and has sound backcountry safety practices.
